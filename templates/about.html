About UNT Food Finder

UNT Food Finder is a student-built platform designed to help the University of North Texas community discover dining options that match their dietary needs, preferences, and budget.

Whether you're looking for vegan, vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, or budget-friendly meals, our platform allows you to filter and explore dining options both on and off campus. Students can view restaurant details, current deals, and save their favorite spots for quick access.
